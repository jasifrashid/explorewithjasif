Meet Jasif

Born and raised in the heart of Kashmir, I've spent years wandering through its meadows, lakes, and mountain passes — not as a tourist, but as someone who calls this paradise home.

My photography and travel stories have reached thousands of travellers who wanted more than a packaged tour. I offer authentic Kashmir: the kind you feel in your bones, not just see through glass.

Whether it's a quiet shikara at dawn on Dal Lake, the first snow on Gulmarg's slopes, or a family afternoon in Nishat Garden — every moment I curate is one I've lived myself.
